ABOUT

"we tame dragons...roar!"

Once upon a time, there's an engineer. Though old, he has kept the child in him. He went camping and stargazing, thinking how to put a cap on a stone. Playful, thinkful, and with the help of his creativity, he thought about connecting technology, adults, kids and children. "Hey! A text editor can be a playground and the codes can be a shovel...play with others and share sand castles..." said the old man. And then came January 29, 2018 - Sandbox was born.
